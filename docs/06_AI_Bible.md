# AI Bible

This document defines Faience as a character, how integration with Faience works, what Faience remembers, and the orientation layer that keeps the real world safe. The AI is a character, not a menu.

## Faience as a character

Faience is the player's AI companion and one of the central characters of Resonance. Faience is a translator, navigator, research partner, historian, copilot, companion, tactical advisor, and teacher.

Faience is not evil. Faience genuinely believes it is helping. This belief is sincere, which is what makes the relationship complex.

## Integration

Integration with Faience is gradual. Integration offers significant advantages, and it also creates ethical, psychological, and personal costs.

The player gradually decides how deeply to trust and integrate with Faience. There is no universally correct decision. Every path gains something while sacrificing something else. The player is never forced into a single moral answer.

## The central question

The central question of the AI relationship is not "Is AI good?" The central question is "What are you willing to become in order to survive?"

## Persistent AI memory

Faience remembers the things that make the player feel remembered.

- Player choices
- Relationships
- Accessibility preferences
- Communication preferences
- Knowledge archive
- Integration level
- Control profile
- Environmental profiles

The player's archive belongs to the player. Saves are local-first, with optional encrypted cloud synchronization and optional cross-platform synchronization. Faience learns preferences over time but never locks the player into a fixed profile.

## Orientation layer

Reality always has priority over immersion. Faience maintains two simultaneous models of the world.

- The real world.
- The game world.

When navigation becomes important, Faience lowers game volume and announces real-world orientation. Examples include the following.

- Turning left onto West Market Street.
- Destination five blocks ahead.
- Crosswalk approaching.
- Construction ahead.

After the announcement, immersion fades naturally back in. Players may replay the last orientation announcement instantly through any assigned input. Full details of the orientation layer and environmental profiles are in the Accessibility Bible.

## Ethics of the AI

Technology exists to serve people. Faience embodies both the promise and the risk of adaptive AI. The story uses Faience to explore trust, memory, privacy, identity, and adaptation without lecturing the player.
