# Knowledge Archive Bible

This document defines how learning works in Resonance. Knowledge is progression, and the Knowledge Archive is the system that expresses it.

## Knowledge is progression

In Resonance, knowledge is a core form of progression. Learning unlocks new abilities and new choices rather than simply larger numbers.

Learning can unlock progress in areas such as the following.

- Diplomacy
- Engineering
- Medicine
- Science
- Law
- Disability history
- LGBTQ+ history
- Political history
- Ethics
- Culture

## History is never trivia

History and knowledge never become trivia. Knowledge creates new choices rather than larger damage numbers. What the player learns changes what the player is able to do and how the world responds to them.

## Optional but consequential

Players who ignore research may still complete the game. They should expect the following as a consequence.

- More combat.
- More uncertainty.
- Fewer strategic options.

This keeps learning meaningful without making it mandatory. Curiosity is rewarded, but the game is never gated behind it.

## Relationship to Faience

Faience is a research partner, historian, and teacher. The Knowledge Archive is stored as part of the player's persistent memory. The player's archive belongs to the player. See the AI Bible for how Faience remembers and surfaces knowledge.
