# Design Notes

This folder holds two kinds of documents: experimental notes that are not yet canon, and approved design notes that are canonical references in their own right.

Experimental notes are a safe place to develop concepts before they mature. Nothing in an experimental note is binding until it is approved and recorded in the Decision Log.

Approved design notes are canon. Detailed chapter designs and the crew progression system are authoritative references that the core Bibles point to rather than duplicate. Each entry below states which kind it is. When an approved note establishes a normative rule, that rule is also recorded in the Decision Log and, where relevant, propagated into the core Bibles.
